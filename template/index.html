Mission
Serve the long-term interests of the American people

The legitimate object of government is to do for a community of people whatever they need to have done, but cannot do at all, or cannot so well do, for themselves.

  • Serve the long-termShort-term thinking is the default. The platform pushes against it.
  • Name who bears the costEvery policy has a tradeoff. We say who pays.
  • Fix systems, not peopleBehavior follows incentives. Change the incentives.
  • Fear and outrage are distractionsThey are tools. We do not use them.
  • Let evidence make the argumentWe present it. You decide.
  • No enemiesPositions, not people. Interests, not villains.
